Introduction back to ToC

The BioC format is designed to act as a maximally expressive, lightweight representation for document annotation. It was developed by a team of collaborators in collaboration with John Wilbur's team at the National Library of Medicine (see http://bioc.sourceforge.org/). Several tools and competitions have been developed for this standard. We here develop a simple port of the core data structure to linked data standards.
